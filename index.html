MEET THE COACH

Dedicated to Your Success

With over 5 years of coaching experience and a passion for developing young athletes, I founded Umbilo Kings basketball to provide personalized, high-quality basketball training that makes a real difference.

My philosophy is simple: every player has unique potential. Through tailored training plans, constant feedback, and a supportive environment, I help students not just improve their skills, but build confidence and a love for the game that lasts a lifetime.
